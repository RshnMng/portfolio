PolyGlot

PolyGlot is the perfect language exchange partner. Simply use it to translate words or sentences between languages or hold a full on conversation in your selected language with the build-in chat bot. Never be too embarrased to practice your language skills again.

Live Link

Project Overview

Learning languages can be difficult - it takes a lot of time and practice. With PolyGlot we provide a language partner that will always be available when you need. Is it 3 in the morning and you want to practice your French? No problem, PolyGlot is there and won't curse you out because its trying to sleep. Are you shy and worried about it taking you 15 minutes to remember how to conjugate that Spanish Verb into the habitual past and annoying your language partner so then he or she ghosts you because the conversation is tedious for them? Never fear! PolyGlot has no concept of time and allows you to take as much time as you need.

With PolyGlot you can practice your aquired language at your leisure. PolyGlot also allows you to search and translate sentences to and from your target language so you can keep the conversation going or just get a quick translation for your personal use. PolyGlot is made for language learners by a language learner!
