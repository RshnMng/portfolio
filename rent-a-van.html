Rent-A-Van

Want to rent a van and explore the great outdoors? You're in luck! This app allows you browse various vans and see which one best suits your needs. Want to rent out your van and make some extra cash? No problem! Create an account, view your dashboard and read reviews.

Live Link

Project Overview

This app replicates a gig app that provides a service. One side of the app allows you to browes available vans and see their details. You can filter the vans by type and see which one would be your perfect van. There is also a host side of the app for those providing a van for rent. The allows you to create an account and actually provide an email and a password which the app will remember and allow you to log into. This side of the app has mock information representing the vans you are currently renting.
